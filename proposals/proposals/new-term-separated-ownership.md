# [new term] Separated ownership

*Paste into a **New term proposal** issue. Term file already drafted at `terms/separated-ownership.yaml` (status: draft).*

**Proposed term:** Separated ownership (Conventionally separated ownership, Severed estate)

**Proposed definition:**

> Ownership of immovable property held separately from ownership of the ground beneath or around it [@gretton_steven_2017]: mineral estates, strata and sub-strata units, airspace parcels, separately held fishings. Separation is effected through conveyancing — a *VoI*/*VoL* composition derives the new ownership object from the principal unit — and the jurisdiction's *numerus clausus* determines which separations are recognised. *Strata title* is the most common instance.

**Why the glossary needs it:**

Strata title is currently the only separation form defined, but it is one instance of a general pattern (minerals, sub-strata, airspace, fishings) whose availability is a numerus clausus question. Source: LRC ontology 'conventionallySeparatedOwnership', 'solumRight', 'strataOwnership', 'subStrataOwnership', grounded in Gretton & Steven (2017).
