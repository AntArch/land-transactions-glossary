**Term:** priority
**Glossary version:** v1.0.0

**Objection / motivation:**

The LRC ontology's 'application' class (synonyms: submission, request) is the mechanism by which ranking is operationally determined; the definition currently describes the relationship but not the mechanism.

**Proposed amendment:**

Append: ranking is commonly determined by date/time of application for registration, which is why presentation queues and caveats bear on priority; add see_also Caveat.
