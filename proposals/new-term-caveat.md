# [new term] Caveat

*Paste into a **New term proposal** issue. Term file already drafted at `terms/caveat.yaml` (status: draft).*

**Proposed term:** Caveat (Caution, Inhibition, Notice)

**Proposed definition:**

> A register object lodged by a party claiming an interest in a *land object*, whose effect is to block or condition the registration of subsequent dealings until the claim is resolved or the caveat lapses. A caveat is not itself a jural incident in the land: it operates on the *admissibility* of later transactions rather than conferring rights, protecting the caveator's claimed position while it is determined. Jurisdictional names vary: caution, inhibition, notice.

**Why the glossary needs it:**

Gives the framework its blocking mechanism: a register object whose effect is on the admissibility of subsequent transactions, connecting the Admissibility principle to Priority. Source: LRC ontology 'caveat' ('prevents the Land Registry from recording any dealing affecting…').
